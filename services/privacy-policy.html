Privacy Policy

SmokeNote  ·  Effective: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 2025

01 / OVERVIEW

Introduction

SmokeNote is a personal cigarette-tracking application built for individuals who want to monitor their smoking habits. Your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is stored, and your rights over that data.

By using SmokeNote, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02 / DATA

Data We Collect

SmokeNote collects only the minimum data needed to function:

  • Cigarette log entries (count + timestamp)
  • Daily and monthly usage patterns derived from your logs
  • Your display name and notification preferences (Settings)
  • Onboarding completion status

We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any biometric data.

03 / STORAGE

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. No data is uploaded to any server or cloud database.

  • Local storage only — no backend database
  • Data never leaves your device by default
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data
04 / THIRD PARTIES

Third-Party Services

SmokeNote does not use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or AI features.

No advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or data brokers are used in this app.

05 / YOUR RIGHTS

Your Rights (PDPA Compliant)

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Right to Access — view all your data in the History and Monthly screens
  • Right to Deletion — use "Clear All Data" in Settings to permanently delete everything
  • Right to Erasure — uninstalling the app removes all stored data
06 / CHILDREN

Children's Privacy

SmokeNote is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used this app, please contact us immediately.

07 / CHANGES

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
